Travel Plans

  1. Manali to Leh (Himalayan Adventure)
    • Distance: ~475 miles
    • Highlights: Rohtang Pass, Keylong, Baralacha La, Pangong Lake, Nubra Valley.
    • Best For: High-altitude adventures, mountain views, and serene lakes.
  2. Mumbai to Goa (Coastal Bliss)
    • Distance: ~430 miles
    • Highlights: Alibaug, Ratnagiri, Ganpatipule, and beaches of Goa.
    • Best For: Coastal views, laid-back vibes, and beach destinations.
  3. Bangalore to Coorg (The Coffee Route)
    • Distance: ~120 miles
    • Highlights: Abbey Falls, Madikeri, Dubare Elephant Camp.
    • Best For: Lush greenery, coffee plantations, and hill stations.
  4. Golden Triangle (Delhi – Agra – Jaipur)
    • Distance: ~450 miles
    • Highlights: Taj Mahal, Jaipur’s Amber Fort, Qutub Minar, Fatehpur Sikri.
    • Best For: History, architecture, and culture.
  5. Kochi to Kovalam (Kerala Backwaters and Beaches)
    • Distance: ~220 miles
    • Highlights: Alleppey, Varkala, and the backwaters of Kerala.
    • Best For: Relaxation, beach resorts, and backwater cruises.
  6. Chennai to Pondicherry (East Coast Road)
    • Distance: ~100 miles
    • Highlights: Mahabalipuram, Auroville, and Pondicherry’s French Quarter.
    • Best For: Coastal roads, colonial heritage, and peaceful vibes.
  7. Udaipur to Kumbhalgarh (Rajputana Route)
    • Distance: ~85 miles
    • Highlights: Kumbhalgarh Fort, Udaipur City Palace, Sajjangarh Palace.
    • Best For: Forts, palaces, and Rajasthan’s royal heritage.
  8. Leh to Srinagar (The Kashmir Highway)
    • Distance: ~300 miles
    • Highlights: Zojila Pass, Sonmarg, Drass, Dal Lake, Mughal Gardens.
    • Best For: Majestic mountains, pristine lakes, and historical significance.
  9. Rishikesh to Haridwar (Spiritual Road Trip)
    • Distance: ~20 miles
    • Highlights: Ganga Aarti, Ram Jhula, Triveni Ghat, Har Ki Pauri.
    • Best For: Spiritual experiences, river views, and sacred sites.
  10. Spiti Valley Road Trip (Himachal Pradesh)
    • Distance: ~250 miles
    • Highlights: Kaza, Key Monastery, Chandratal Lake, Nako Village.
    • Best For: Seclusion, Tibetan culture, and high-altitude deserts.
